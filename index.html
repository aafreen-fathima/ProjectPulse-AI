How it works

Monday morning in 15 minutes

What used to take 2 hours of manual work — done and delivered before the first standup.

8:00am

System ingests overnight data automated

JIRA webhooks, SQL budget actuals, and SharePoint docs are pulled and merged into a unified pandas DataFrame.

8:01am

XGBoost scores every deliverable AI · XGBoost

50+ features: task age, velocity trend, dependency depth, sentiment score. Two projects flagged at >65% slippage probability.

8:02am

Gap analysis computes variances automated

Schedule variance, budget variance, and scope creep delta computed in pandas and written to Supabase.

8:03am

Claude API drafts the executive report AI · Claude

Plotly charts + structured metrics fed into the narrative agent → 600-word branded executive summary with headline finding, risk escalation, and recommended actions.

8:05am

PMO lead reviews and approves 1 click

Dashboard shows risk flags with SHAP explanations, a drafted stakeholder email, and mitigation recommendations. Approve with one click.

8:15am

Report delivered to all stakeholders automated

Resend emails the PDF + PowerPoint deck to the exec list. Supabase audit log updated. SharePoint archive synced. Done.
